📋 BASIC SERVICE INFORMATION

Service Name

Dyslexia Intelligence Monitoring System (DIMS) — Intelligent Dyslexia Monitoring and Intervention System

Main Objective

identify, monitor and Intervention in Children (6-17 years) with developmental dyslexia through a automated system Diagnosis continuous, specialized cognitive tests and geractionf plans of Intervention personalized baseados in Intelligence artificial, garantindo detection precoce, longitudinal Monitoring and compliance with LGPD.

Description executive

The DIMS is a platform of 9 stages that automates o complete cycle of Monitoring in dyslexia:

  1. Initial Identification → Symptom collectionf alert (feedback of educatores)
  2. screening Comportamental → Questionnaire of dyslexia (DSM-5 aligned)
  3. Assessment Cognitive assisted → Batteries of Tests automated (WPM, fluency, comprehension)
  4. Phonological Processing Analysis → Detectionf specific deficit
  5. Geractionf Preliminary Diagnosis → Multidimensional scores with AI
  6. Development of Individualized Plan → pedagogicalgical recommendationsgical by Hermes Agent
  7. Multi-channel Notification → Communication with all stakeholders
  8. continuous Monitoring → Periodic tests with Progress Analysis
  9. Assessment of Output → impact of the Intervention and future recommendations

Tests Specific (STAGE 3 - Assessment Cognitive assisted)

BATTERY of Tests MULTIDIMENSIONAL FOR DYSLEXIA 1. processing PHONOLOGICAL (Weight 40%)  ├─ Task of Awareness Phonemic  │ └─ identify, isolate, manipular phonemes initial/final  ├─ Test of Rhyme Implicit  │ └─ Recognize patterns of rhyme (estimated vs. real)  ├─ Segmentation Syllabic  │ └─ Dividir words in syllables (production + identificaction)  └─ Reversion of Syllables  └─ Reorganizar syllables in sequence reversa 2. speed and FLUENCY of Reading (Weight 35%)  ├─ Test WPM (Words Per Minute)  │ ├─ passage of Test-retest of 1 minute  │ ├─ Comparison with normal by age  │ └─ Contagem of errors (omissions, insertions, substitutions)  ├─ Reading of No-words (Pseudopalavras)  │ └─ Capacity of aplicar regras phonologicals to stimuli novos  ├─ Reading of Words Irregulares  │ └─ Capacity of Recognition visual (whole-word)  └─ Time of Nomeaction Fast (RAN - Rapid Automatized Naming)  └─ Speed of Access to the lexicon 3. comprehension of Reading (Weight 15%)  ├─ Questions of multiple choice about passage read  ├─ Inference and connection of ideas  ├─ Retention of details (literal vs. inferential)  └─ Comparison: comprehension Reading vs. comprehension auditory 4. processing visual-ORTHOGRAPHIC (Weight 10%)  ├─ Test of Choice visual (choose word correct between similar)  ├─ Memory of sequence visual  ├─ Symmetry and orientation of Letters  └─ Recognition of standard Orthographic TIMING and sequence of the Tests ├─ Session 1 (10-15 min): Awareness Phonemic + RAN ├─ Session 2 (10-15 min): WPM + Pseudo-words ├─ Session 3 (10-15 min): comprehension + Processing visual └─ Total: ~40-45 minutes in 3 sessions (reduces fatigue)

🎯 JUSTIFICATION of the CHOICES OF DESIGN

by that 9 stages?

  • 7 stages original eram insufficient to capture the complexity of the dyslexia
  • stages 8-9 added for continuous Monitoring and Assessment of long-term
  • Dyslexia requires longitudinal Monitoring (is not Diagnosis unique, but evolution)

by that Tests Multidimensional?

  • Dyslexia is not unidimensional: involves phonology, fluency, comprehension, processing visual
  • A single metric (WPM) is insufficient: create false negatives
  • Audiovisual Discrepancy is key marker to differentiate dyslexia of the deficit of general Language

by that Score Ponderado?

  • weights reflect neuroscience: Phonological Processing (40%) is the prhymery deficit in dyslexia
  • Fluency (35%) and comprehension (15%) capture functional impact
  • Processing visual (10%) identify subtypes

by that Plan Personalized by AI?

  • standard of deficits varies child to child
  • AI can generate recommendations specifics based in the perfil multidimensional
  • avoids abordagem generic one-size-fits-all
