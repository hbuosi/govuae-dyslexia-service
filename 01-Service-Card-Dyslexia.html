📋 BASIC SERVICE INFORMATION

Service Name

Dyslexia Intelligence Monitoring System (DIMS) — Intelligent Dyslexia Monitoring and Intervention System

Main Objective

identify, monitor and Intervention in Children (6-17 anos) with developmental dyslexia through to system automated Diagnosis continuous, specialized cognitive tests and geraction of plans of Intervention personalized baseados in Intelligence artificial, garantindo detection precoce, Monitoring longitudinal and compliance with LGPD.

Description executive

O DIMS é uma plataforma of 9 stages that automates o complete cycle of Monitoring in dyslexia:

  1. Initial Identification → Symptom collection of alert (feedback of educatores)
  2. screening Comportamental → Questionnaire of dyslexia (DSM-5 aligned)
  3. Assessment Cognitive assisted → Baterias of Tests automatizadas (WPM, fluency, comprehension)
  4. Phonological Processing Analysis → Detection of specific deficit
  5. Geraction of Preliminary Diagnosis → Multidimensional scores with AI
  6. Development of Individualized Plan → recommendations pedagógicas by Hermes Agent
  7. Multi-channel Notification → Communication with all stakeholders
  8. continuous Monitoring → Periodic tests with Progress Analysis
  9. Assessment of Output → impact of the Intervention and future recommendations

Tests Specific (STAGE 3 - Assessment Cognitive assisted)

BATERIA of Tests MULTIDIMENSIONAL FOR DYSLEXIA 1. processing FONOLÓGICO (Weight 40%)  ├─ Task of Consciência Phonemic  │ └─ identify, isolate, manipular fonemas iniciais/finais  ├─ Test of Rima Implícita  │ └─ Reconhecer padrões of rima (estimado vs. real)  ├─ Segmentation Silábica  │ └─ Dividir words in sílabas (production + identificaction)  └─ Reversion of Sílabas  └─ Reorganizar sílabas in sequence reversa 2. speed and FLUÊNCIA of Reading (Weight 35%)  ├─ Test WPM (Words Per Minute)  │ ├─ passage of Test-reteste of 1 minute  │ ├─ Comparison with normal by age  │ └─ Contagem of erros (omissões, inserções, substituições)  ├─ Reading of Não-words (Pseudopalavras)  │ └─ Capacity of aplicar regras phonologicals to estímulos novos  ├─ Reading of Words Irregulares  │ └─ Capacity of Recognition visual (whole-word)  └─ Time of Nomeaction Rápida (RAN - Rapid Automatized Naming)  └─ Speed of Access to the lexicon 3. comprehension of Reading (Weight 15%)  ├─ Questions of multiple choice about passage read  ├─ Inference and connection of ideas  ├─ Retention of details (literal vs. inferential)  └─ Comparison: comprehension Reading vs. comprehension auditory 4. processing visual-ORTOGRÁFICO (Weight 10%)  ├─ Test of Choice visual (choose word correct between similar)  ├─ Memory of sequence visual  ├─ Symmetry and orientation of Letters  └─ Recognition of standard Ortográfico TIMING and sequence of the Tests ├─ Sessão 1 (10-15 min): Consciência Phonemic + RAN ├─ Sessão 2 (10-15 min): WPM + Pseudo-words ├─ Sessão 3 (10-15 min): comprehension + Processing visual └─ Total: ~40-45 minutes in 3 sessões (reduces fatigue)

📊 Diagnosis DIMENSIONS (Multidimensional Profile)

Phonological Score (0-100) ├─ 0-30: Normal - Phonological Processing adequate ├─ 31-60: Intermediário - Slight Difficulty, possible preventive Intervention ├─ 61-85: Risk Moderate - significant deficit, Monitoring recommended └─ >85: Alto Risk of Dyslexia - urgent Intervention recommended SCORE OF FLUÊNCIA (0-100) ├─ 0-30: Very Slow - WPM > 2 SDs below of the normal for age ├─ 31-60: Lentidão Moderada - WPM 1-2 SDs below ├─ 61-85: Slightly Below of the Norma - WPM próximo à média └─ >85: Fluência Adequate SCORE OF comprehension (0-100) ├─ 0-30: deficitário - comprehension significantly reduced ├─ 31-60: Below of the Esperado - Difficulty in extract meaning ├─ 61-85: Adequate - comprehension literal preserved └─ >85: Above of the Esperado - Good Access to the meaning SCORE OF processing visual (0-100) ├─ 0-30: deficitário ├─ 31-60: Intermediário ├─ 61-100: Adequate ÍNDICE OF DISCREPÂNCIA AUDIOvisual (Marker important) ├─ Cálculo: |comprehension Auditory - comprehension Reading| ├─ >15 points: Strong indicator of dyslexia específica (processing of Reading vs. Language) └─ <15 points: Possível deficit of general Language

🎯 JUSTIFICATION of the CHOICES OF DESIGN

by that 9 stages?

  • 7 stages original eram insufficient to capture the complexity of the dyslexia
  • stages 8-9 added for continuous Monitoring and Assessment of long-term
  • Dyslexia requires Monitoring longitudinal (não é Diagnosis único, mas evolution)

by that Tests Multidimensionais?

  • Dyslexia não é unidimensional: involves phonology, fluency, comprehension, processing visual
  • Uma única metric (WPM) é insufficient: create false negatives
  • Discrepância audiovisual é key marker to differentiate dyslexia of the deficit of general Language

by that Score Ponderado?

  • weights reflect neurociência: Phonological Processing (40%) é deficit primário in dyslexia
  • Fluência (35%) and comprehension (15%) capture functional impact
  • Processing visual (10%) identify subtypes

by that Plan Personalized by AI?

  • standard of deficits varies child to child
  • AI can generate recommendations específicas based in the perfil multidimensional
  • avoids abordagem genérica one-size-fits-all
