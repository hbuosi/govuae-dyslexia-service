ESCALATION PATHS

Level 1: Operational Issues (ONG Coordinator Resolution)

  • SLA breaches <4 hours
  • Individual case delays
  • Staff capacity issues
  • Escalation Trigger: Unresolved after 48 hours
  • Escalate To: Service Delivery Board

Level 2: Process/System Issues (Service Delivery Board)

  • Systemic process failures
  • Data quality issues
  • Integration problems
  • Escalation Trigger: Affecting >10% of cases or impacting 3+ ONGs
  • Escalate To: Executive Steering Committee

Level 3: Strategic Issues (Executive Committee)

  • Policy conflicts
  • Funding allocation
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Multi-state coordination
  • Escalation Trigger: >1 week unresolved or affects policy
  • Escalate To: Service Ministers

Level 4: Critical/Urgent (Same-Day Escalation)

  • Child safety/abuse indicators
  • Data breaches
  • System outages >4 hours
  • LGPD violations
  • Immediate Action: Direct to Service Board + Legal + Compliance

RACI QUALITY CHECKS

Every activity has exactly 1 "Accountable" owner. Responsible parties are skilled/trained for their role. Consulted parties have direct expertise contribution. Informed parties receive updates on outcomes. No circular dependencies (A doesn't depend on their own R). All critical handoffs have clear accountability. Escalation paths are unambiguous. SLAs align with responsible party capabilities.
